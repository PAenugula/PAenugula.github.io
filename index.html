PROJECTS

Oddjob

One of my first projects, Oddjob was an "Uber for chores". It allowed users to post their household tasks that they don't have time for or can't do themselves on a map. Other users can pick up the job. The app implemented a dynamic map user interface with a custom in-app messaging system.

Stock Betr

As part of a project for Blockchain at Calpoly, we created a betting website using cryptocurrency. It was built on the Ethereum blockchain using the Web3js framework and solidity contracts.

Loconote

A product of my first hackathon, SBhacks, Loconote is a simple yet fun app that lets users post their emotions along with a note on the map. Other users can see what people think around them by seeing the posts within a certain radius.

Spotifai

My team and I at SLOHacks created an app that finds the right song for you. Using Google's computer vision and machine learning API, we evaluate your current mood using a selfie and direct you to a song that brightens your day.
